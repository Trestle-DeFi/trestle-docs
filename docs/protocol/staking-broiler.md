# BroilerPlus LP Mining

## The Liquidity Anchor Token

**BroilerPlus (BRT)** has been active on Polygon since 2021, providing the **deep liquidity foundation** for the Trestle ecosystem.

> **⚠️ BRT Transfer Tax:** BRT has a **5% transfer tax** enforced at the token-contract level (not a protocol fee). Staking contracts automatically gross up rewards so users receive the full intended amount without deduction.

### Staking Mechanics

Stake **BRT/WPOL LP tokens** to earn:
- **BroilerPlus rewards**
- **Governance Points (xGov)**

#### Lock Periods

| Lock Period | Multiplier |
|-------------|------------|
| 6 months | 1.4× |
| 12 months | 1.6× |
| 18 months | 1.8× |

#### Early Unstake Penalty
- **24-hour lockdown** before early unstake
- **50% of rewards** + **50% of gov points** penalty

### Referral Bonus

- **5% bonus** on both rewards and governance points
- Referral percentage is configurable (capped at 20%)

### BRT Fee Gross-Up

The contract accounts for BRT's 5% transfer fee, ensuring accurate reward distribution.

### Acquiring BRT/WPOL LP

1. Acquire BRT tokens from QuickSwap
2. Acquire WPOL tokens (wrapped MATIC)
3. Add liquidity on QuickSwap to receive LP tokens
4. Stake LP tokens in the BroilerPlus staking contract

### Governance Points (xGov)

Earned through LP mining, xGov points:
- Convert to Governance Tokens after Vault activation
- Grant voting rights on protocol upgrades
- Require BRT/WPOL LP commitment to earn
