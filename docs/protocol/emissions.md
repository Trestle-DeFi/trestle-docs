## Governance-Driven Emissions

The reward rate per block is determined by **Governance Token Holders**:

- **Standard Mode**: Rewards based on current LP pool size to maintain target APY
- **Boost Mode**: Temporarily increase emissions to bootstrap new pools (e.g., Marketplace launch)
- **Taper Mode**: Reduce emissions as protocol matures to preserve mining pool
- **Safeguard**: Emissions stop when pool exhausted; new tokens require Governance vote

## Why Dynamic?

Allows Trestle to adapt to market conditions:
- Lower emissions in bear markets to conserve
- Increase in bull markets to attract liquidity
- Ensures sustainability for **10+ years**
