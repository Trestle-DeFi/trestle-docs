# Protocol Overview

## The Trestle Ecosystem

### The Three-Token System

| Section | Token | Purpose | Utility |
|---------|-------|---------|---------|
| **Stake** | hNOBT | Community Growth | Distributed via Telegram Mini-App for tasks, referrals, early engagement. Can be spent on **digital goods** or used to "boost" freelancer profiles. |
| **Mine** | BroilerPlus | Liquidity Anchor | Stake BRT/WPOL LP to earn BroilerPlus rewards and Governance Points. Active on Polygon since 2021. BRT has a **5% transfer tax** at the token-contract level. |
| **Vault** | Governance Token | Value Capture | Earns percentage of **marketplace transaction fees** and grants voting rights. Earned exclusively through Vault staking. |

## The Marketplace: Empowering Freelancers and Digital Creators

Trestle Platform is a **decentralized, multi-sided marketplace** designed to empower **freelancers, digital creators, and buyers** with **secure, instant, and low-fee transactions**.

### Key Features for Freelancers

- **Secure Milestone Escrow**: Smart contracts hold funds until work is verified
- **Instant Payments**: No banking delays
- **Profile Boosting**: Spend hNOBT to increase visibility
- **Digital Goods Marketplace**: NFTs, templates, software with zero middlemen
- **AI-Powered Matching**: Freelancer-job matching based on skills and reputation
- **AI Fraud Detection**: Real-time monitoring prevents Sybil attacks

## Real-World Assets (RWA): Future Expansion

While Trestle begins with **digital goods**, it is designed to bridge with **real-world assets**:

- **Tokenize fractional shares** of real estate, bonds
- **Trade RWA tokens** on the marketplace
- **Earn passive income** from asset yields

**Current Status**: Infrastructure planned (Oracle, AI verification). Launch scheduled post-Mainnet Marketplace (Phase 4).
