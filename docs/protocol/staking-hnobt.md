# hNOBT Staking

## The Gateway Token

The **hNOBT** (Community Growth Token) serves as the entry point to the Trestle ecosystem.

### Distribution Methods

- **Telegram Mini-App** - Complete tasks, earn hNOBT
- **Referrals** - Invite friends, earn bonuses
- **Early Engagement** - Beta testers and contributors

### Staking Mechanics

#### Lock Periods

| Lock Period | Multiplier |
|-------------|------------|
| 3 months | 1.0× |
| 6 months | 1.25× |
| 12 months | 1.5× |

#### Early Unstake Penalty
- **24-hour lockdown** period before early unstake allowed
- **50% penalty** on rewards for early withdrawal

### Utility

- **Spend on digital goods** in the marketplace
- **Boost freelancer profiles** for increased visibility
- **Stake to earn BroilerPlus rewards**

### Phase 1 Status

- **Untradable**: Cannot be sold on DEXs until Phase 2 (Marketplace) launch
- **Supply**: Dynamic/Massminted via tasks (no hard cap on issuance)
- **Earning**: Exclusively through Telegram Reward Hub

### Future Integration

- hNOBT will be convertible to BroilerPlus via the Virtual Vault
- Governance Points earned through Vault staking (requires BRT/WPOL LP)
