# Real-World Assets (RWA) Roadmap

While Trestle begins with digital goods, the long-term vision is to bridge **DeFi** with **Real-World Assets**.

## Phase 4: RWA Tokenization (Future)
- **Fractional Ownership:** Tokenize real estate, treasury bonds, and commodities.
- **Liquidity Pools:** Provide liquidity for RWA tokens and earn staking rewards.
- **Yield Generation:** Earn passive income from rental yields or bond interest.

## Compliance & Custody
- **Legal Framework:** Partnerships with licensed custodians to ensure global regulatory compliance.
- **Oracle Verification:** Chainlink Oracles for real-time asset valuation.
- **AI Verification:** Document analysis to prevent fraud in asset backing.

## Timeline
- **Q3 2026:** Infrastructure planning (Oracles, Custody).
- **Q4 2026:** Testnet RWA pilot (Simulated assets).
- **2027:** Mainnet RWA launch (Subject to regulatory approval).

> **Note:** RWA features are **not yet live**. Current focus is on stabilizing the Digital Goods Marketplace and Governance Token activation.
