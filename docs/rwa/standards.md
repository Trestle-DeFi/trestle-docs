# RWA Standards & Compliance

Trestle adheres to industry standards for tokenizing real-world assets.

## Technical Standards
- **ERC-3643 (T-REX):** For compliance-aware token transfers (Whitelisting).
- **ERC-1400:** Security token standards.
- **Chainlink Proof of Reserve:** Ensuring on-chain tokens are backed 1:1 by off-chain assets.

## Regulatory Compliance
- **KYC/AML:** Mandatory for RWA participants.
- **Geofencing:** Assets may be restricted to specific jurisdictions based on local laws.
- **Audit Trail:** All RWA transactions are immutable and auditable on-chain.
